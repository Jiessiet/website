About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Toronto, advised by Prof. Shurui Zhou in ForColab. My area of research focuses on understanding human cognition through different means such as computational modeling, behavioral experiments, and empirical methods.

I did both my undergrad and master's at the University of Toronto in Computer Science, where I also learned about Cognitive Science as an interdiciplinary field, specialising in Computer Science and computations.

I love Toronto summers, finding cool technologies, and the arts.
